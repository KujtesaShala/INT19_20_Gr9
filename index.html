"SPRING OF WATER"

The name of the city could be
 derived from Proto-Slavic dialectal
 word *pryščina,meaning 
 "spring (of water)", 
 ...
 which is also attested 
 in the Moravian 
 dialects of Czech;
 it is derived from
 the verb *pryskati, meaning 
 "to splash" or "to spray"
