Soma Book Station

For a cosy atmosphere, quality music (very often live), a wide selection of fun cocktails, various tastes of Mediterranean food and more - head over to Soma Book Station nearby sheshi Skënderbe!

Dit' e Nat'

The first bookshop café in Prishtina, Dit' e Nat', is literally meant for Days & Nights. With a friendly co-working space, great food to keep you full (vegan too) and plenty of cultural events in the evenings, it's not hard to understand the reason behind the name and why it has stolen the hearts of so many here in Prishtina.

Sonder

If you look up "sonder" in the dictionary, it will remind you that everyone lives their own complex and vivid lives, just like you. If you check out Sonder in Prishtina however, you will find a cozy café, a place you can go to deal with life's complexities over a cup of latte and delicious bagels.

Shpija e Vjetër

A homey interior with old mix-and-matched ornaments and delicious food just like your grandma would make. But remember, at Shpija e Vjetër there is no grandma to over-feed you, you are a grown-up and can decide for yourself. That's a good thing, sometimes.

Casa Rita Restaurant

Casa Rita is one of the greatest restaurants in Prishtina! Great ambient, cozy interior, 15 minutes from downtown, professional staff and most importantly amazing food!! Either eating or drinking solo, go with your loved one or a family gathering Casa Rita is the place to go on casual or more formal dining place. Food quality is beyond what most places offer in Prishtina. Variety of wines, spirits and beers is also another reason why one should try this restaurant. This one is a must see place if you’re in Prishtina even for a day.

HANNAH nё park

You know you're in for a treat when you sit down at an Albanian dinner table! Traditional pies and other baked goods, homemade spreads, cheeses, fresh and pickled vegetables, are all placed in the middle of the table for all to help themselves. The Albanian way of dining is not only a delight for the tummy, but a bonding experience between friends & family members‍, sharing stories the same way the food is shared amongst them. In a way, we learn to share things as soon as we learn to bite. And that is beautiful. Head down to Hannah ne park, a cute cafe/restaurant located in the middle of Taukbashqe park, to get a first-hand experience of the Albanian dinner table.
